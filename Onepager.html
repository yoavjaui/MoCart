What is MOCART?

MOCART is a VR platform in the grocery industry. MOCART utilizes VR technology to allow end consumer remote supermarket purchase.

Contact the entrepreneurs

Value proposition

Experience

Simulate supermarket in the VR app, accurate simulation of the products that illustrates their size and allows you to read the value page on the back of the product.

Convenience

V-commerce saves us waiting in lines and wasting time shopping in the physical supermarket and in addition reduces the inconvenience of online shopping.

Metaverse

The platform will lead the shopping experience in Metaverse, an area that is growing at a tremendous rate and will change the form of shopping as we know it today.
By MOCART, supermarkets thrive in an omnichannel world and remain groceries stay relevant in the future.

The Opportunity

The stability of the grocery market combined with the exponential growth of the VR market
VR Market Size

The industry as a whole is growing at a fast pace, with the global VR market size projected to increase from less than seven billion U.S. dollars in 2022 to more than 12 billion U.S. dollars by 2024.

Grocery Market Size

In 2020, U.S. supermarket and grocery store sales amounted to about 741.6 billion U.S. dollars. This is more than double the sales amounts that were generated back in 1992.

Metaverse

Metaverse regarded as the next iteration of the internet, the metaverse is where the physical and digital worlds come together.
